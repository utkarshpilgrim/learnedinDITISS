# Services 

Service is the delivery of the value to the customer, by facilitating the customer want to achieve, without the ownnershiop of the specific cost. For example, setting up an email server. 

Providing solution needs a proper inspection, like an email server, you need to understand the whole story, where you need to inspect on all the details about the problems that your customer might face, the customer is not bothered about the problem, so it is the service provider who need to to analyse the requirements first and then provide the solutions. 

Service has two ends, one is the provider and another is consumer/customer, meaning IT department when providing the service to departments within the organosation, it is called **internal service**, because the service is getting implemented within the organisation, but when the service is meant for someone/org outside the company, it is called **external service**. 

This is called a recurring task, because the ITIL gives the standards that needs to be implemented again and again. Following requirements, research the knowledge centre, where department follows the same standards for multiple services that the organisation is using. It can be different *types* of services, the service management can be taken care of. 

Three aspects/types of services.

Delivering the basic outcomes required by one or more customers, meaning when we talk about the core service, say email service, setting up email service we need to look for three core services, such as smtp, pop/imap servers, so these are called the core service, meaning setting up a smtp server and pop/imap server is crucial in order to organsiation make communication using the emails. 

- **Core service** - In this, email service becomes the core service, meaning the custoemer doesn't know that behind the scene it is using the smtp and pop/imap service.

- **Enabling service** - when it comes to enabling teh actual implemetation of the services, where we actually put the servers into action. From ITIL perspective the email service is using the smtp and pop/imap servers, which becomes and are considered at the enabling service. 

- **Enhancing service** - the service that will make the services look more appealing, such as thunderbird, roundcube is the one service that comes under the enhancing the services.
